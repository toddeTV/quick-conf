# Call for Papers (CFP)

## What is a CFP?

A Call for Papers (CFP) is an open invitation for speakers to submit talk proposals for our conference. If you have expertise in a topic that would interest our audience, we'd love to hear from you!

## How to Submit

We use **Sessionize** to manage our Call for Papers. Sessionize is a professional platform that makes it easy to submit and manage talk proposals.

To submit your talk proposal:

1. Visit our CFP page on Sessionize
2. Create an account or log in
3. Fill out the submission form with your talk details
4. Submit before the deadline
